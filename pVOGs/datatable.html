Genome Table - Information regarding all prokaryotic virus genomes analyzed in the pVOGs database:

* NOTE: The following virus genomic data was current as of May 2016. However, taxonomic nomenclature of viruses changes over time (e.g., Taxonomy of prokaryotic viruses: update from the ICTV bacterial and archaeal viruses subcommittee ➚), and already these names no longer reflect the current status as approved by the International Committee on Taxonomy of Viruses ➚. Other data associated with these genomes such as gene annotations (functional information, start/stop coordinates, metadata, or even their presence/absence in a genome) may also change over time. Clicking on the link of species name or genome accession will redirect you to the NCBI Taxonomy ➚ or NCBI Nucleotide ➚ database, respectively, containing the most up-to-date data that NCBI has available.
